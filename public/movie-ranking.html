The Best Movies According to Sab

My favourite movies so far this year

A Man Called Otto

This is an excellent movie. Humourous, sad and a moving example of friendship and love.

The Best of Me

A very entertaining love story that has a backbone to it and isn't too sappy.

Work It

If you like hip hop dance movies, this is a pretty good one. Predictable but enjoyed the dancing - made me want to get up and dance.

Murdery Mystery 2

Can't say too much about this other than I'm glad I watched it on Netfilx and didn't pay to see it in a movie theatre. Watch when you want to see watch something absolutely rediculous!
