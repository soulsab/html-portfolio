The Best Movies According to Sab

My top 3 movies of all-time

A Man Called Otto

This is a great movie about frientship, love and acceptance for our neighbours

Philadelphia Store

There a 2 versions of this movie: black and white version with Katherine Hepburn and Cary Grant and the musical version with Grace Kelly and Bing Crosby. I like the musical version for the songs but love the back and white version for Cary Grant - he's so damn dapper!

Barbie

I LOVED Barbie as a kid but I can't say I loved the movie - definitely wouldn't pay $16 to see in the theatre. Not sure what it was but . . . despite my confusion, I would definitely watch this movie again.
